Partners HealthCare International (PHI) is the global advisory services arm of Partners HealthCare, the leading integrated academic health care system in the United States. Partners HealthCareis home to five Harvard Medical School-affiliated teaching hospitals, including Brigham and Women's Hospital, Massachusetts General Hospital, Massachusetts Eye and Ear, McLean Hospital, Spaulding Rehabilitation Network and, our community hospitals, including Newton-Wellesley Hospital and North Shore Medical Center. Over the past two decades, PHI has partnered with institutions and health care systems in more than 40 countries to build local health care capacity and drive transformative, sustainable improvements so that communities across the globe have access to an ever-higher standard of care. PHI is a division of PartnersInnovation.

PHI works with global clients and partners who share our vision of bringing modern, high-quality health care to international communities. We look forward to helping partners build leading brands and creating local solutions based on international standards.
